My Skills

I've always considered it important to know what a person is like, in addition to their background so here are some of my strengths as a person...

Communication and cooperation skills

I have been told that I am a good leader in projects because I get along with everyone and I am usually the first to propose when making decisions. When working as a group, I think that it is important to get everyone on board as quickly as possible and that is why I encourage others to come forward and introduce themselves and also tell about their thoughts. I am always willing to hear about how others think because it helps with decision making. As a person, I think that I am more than happy to communicate with others and take part in collaborative tasks.

Adaptability and learning skills

I am always willing to learn new things and develop further so that I can be the best version of myself and that is why I have received feedback from previous jobs that I am a quick learner and I also adapt well to the work environment. I am a person that thinks that studies are just the beginning and you will learn more than you could have ever imagined and that is why I strive to adapt to all kinds of situations and learn something new from them.

Persistent and optimistic skills

As a person, I am optimistic about the things I face, and that optimism includes persistency. If I encounter exhausting or difficult tasks, I approach them with a positive attitude and critical thinking. Giving up is not an option for me because there is a solution to every problem and with the right attitude they are usually found. I think that problems should be dealt with when they arise before they pile up and that is why I am a person, who doesn't give up easily.
